## 📁 Featured Projects

### 🌧️ [Rainfall & El Niño Time Series Analysis](./01_rainfall_timeseries)
> **R** · Time Series · ARIMA · SARIMAX · Forecasting

Modeled 70+ years of Indian monthly rainfall data (1950–2021) and quantified the influence of the El Niño (ONI) index using SARIMAX. Achieved **15–25% lower RMSE** than baseline ARIMA — with outputs applicable to agricultural planning.

### 🧪 [Study Methods & Cognitive Focus — 2³ Factorial Experiment](./02_factorial_experiment)
> **R** · Design of Experiments · ANOVA · Interaction Effects

Designed and executed a full 2³ factorial experiment (n=108) examining how study method, location, and peer interaction affect concentration scores. Identified **Digital + Group** as the optimal combination — **15–25% above baseline**.

---

### 💰 [Census Income Prediction — Logistic Regression](./03_census_income)
> **Python** · scikit-learn · Classification · EDA

Built a logistic regression classifier on the UCI Adult Census dataset (~32K records) to predict income > $50K. Achieved **~85% accuracy** and **AUC ~0.88**, with Random Forest comparison and feature importance analysis.

### 🏏 [IPL Match Score Analysis (2008–2023)](./04_ipl_analysis)
> **R** · Sports Analytics · Regression · EDA

Analyzed 16 seasons of IPL ball-by-ball data: team performance, toss impact, phase-wise run rates, and player statistics. Built a **multiple linear regression model** predicting first-innings totals (R² ~0.65, RMSE ~18 runs).
